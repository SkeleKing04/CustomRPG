MoveSOs will have the
	type of move
	damage
	effects
	projectiles
	and cores
the type of move will determine what the move does
the types of moves are
	melee
		close ranged simple hitbox attack
		ie - sword swing
	physicsRanged
		fires a projectile affected by gravity
		ie - fire arrow
	Ranged
		fires a projectile unaffected by gravity
		ie - fireball or laser
	Boost
		Changes value of something (usually a player stat)
		ie - speed boost or heal
	Summon/Cast
		Summons something into existance
		ie - walls or minions
moves may not have damage, effects or projectiles
cores might add damage, effects of projectiles

TAKING DAMAGE

the damage is calculated attacking side, so the damage is sent to the defender
